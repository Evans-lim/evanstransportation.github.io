Introduction

Evans Transportation Pte Ltd was established at 1980 and has become a major bus operator in Singapore. Evans Transportation is the reliable choice for various transport requirements. We have an experienced management staff, experienced management staff, strong fleet of showcase vehicles and excellent operations system.

Evans Transportation has a bus for every occasion. Our fleet has grown to 150 buses covering well known vehicle brands like Isuzu, Toyota and King Long. The average age of our bus fleet is less than five years and we keep our each bus well maintained. Regular in house maintenance keeps our buses at their top condition.
